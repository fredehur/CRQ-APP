# AeroGrid Wind Solutions — Risk Landscape at a Glance

## Who We Are

AeroGrid Wind Solutions is a global renewable energy company operating in 5 regions across 4 continents.

- **Business split:** 75% Wind Turbine Manufacturing / 25% Global Service & Maintenance
- **Workforce:** ~30,000 employees worldwide
- **Regions:** APAC, AME (North America), LATAM, MED (Mediterranean), NCE (Northern & Central Europe)

---

## Our Crown Jewels — What Adversaries Target

These are the four assets that define AeroGrid's competitive advantage. Losing them means losing the business.

1. **Proprietary turbine aerodynamic designs and material IP** — the engineering know-how that makes our blades more efficient than competitors
2. **OT/SCADA networks in global blade manufacturing plants** — the operational technology that controls physical production lines
3. **Real-time predictive maintenance algorithms** — software that detects failures before they happen, powering our service contracts
4. **Live telemetry data from offshore and onshore wind farms** — streaming sensor data from thousands of turbines globally

## The Critical Pattern

All three escalated threats independently target the same surface: turbine operational technology and predictive systems — the same assets in AeroGrid's crown jewel register. This is a compound risk posture, not three isolated incidents. A disruption to any one escalated region independently affects AeroGrid's ability to manufacture turbines and deliver service contracts on time.

**Global velocity: Stable** — this compound posture has held for 5 consecutive pipeline runs. It is entrenched, not transient.
