## Key Insights From the Data

### The Frequency vs. Impact Disconnect

The most common threat is NOT the most costly:
- **System intrusion** is the most frequent (43.3% of all incidents) but ranks only #3 by financial impact
- **Ransomware** is less frequent (31.7%) but is the #1 financial threat — responsible for 37.6% of all dollar losses
- **Accidental disclosure** happens less often than system intrusion but causes nearly as much financial damage (#2)

### The Rare-but-Devastating Pattern

- Ransomware and Accidental disclosure together = **73.7% of all financial losses** from only **47.6% of incidents**
- Insider misuse causes 4.4% of losses from only 2.5% of incidents — disproportionate financial damage per event
- The bottom 6 scenarios combined cause less than 1% of financial losses

### What This Means for AeroGrid

All three of AeroGrid's currently escalated regions map to top-4 financial impact scenarios:
- **AME:** Ransomware (#1 financial impact) — $22M exposure
- **APAC:** System intrusion (#3 financial impact) — $18.5M exposure
- **MED:** Insider misuse (#4 financial impact) — $4.2M exposure

The pipeline's escalation logic is calibrated to this data: only scenarios ranking in the top 4 by financial impact trigger full board-level analysis. LATAM and NCE produced no signals matching top-4 scenarios — confirmed clear.
