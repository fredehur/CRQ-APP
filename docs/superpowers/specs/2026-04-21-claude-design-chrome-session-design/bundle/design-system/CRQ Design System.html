01.1 · FOUNDATIONS
Brand & Principles
CRQ is a threat-intelligence practice. Its artifacts are read under pressure — by executives deciding this week, not this quarter. Every design choice below serves signal over ornament.
P·01
Signal over ornament
No gradients, no decorative imagery, no emoji. Color exists to encode severity; whitespace exists to separate signal.
P·02
Print-report density
Dense enough that a CISO can absorb a page in 60 seconds. Loose enough that the board can read it on a screen from six feet.
P·03
Structured evidence
Every claim travels with its confidence: Admiralty grade, source count, refresh time. Meta-rails are not optional.

04.1 · USAGE
Do / Don't
The most common ways the system is broken, paired with the correct approach. If an output looks different from everything here, it's almost certainly one of these.
Do — Pill leads, severity owned
CRITICAL · MED · NEW
Eastern Mediterranean shadow-fleet activity confirmed
Pill band carries the severity grade, region, and flag — once — above the title. Title stays navy.
Don't — Recolor the title
Eastern Mediterranean shadow-fleet activity confirmed
CRITICAL · MED · NEW
Severity color lives in the pill and the dot. Not in the headline. This double-encodes and reduces scan-ability.
Do — Meta rail with evidence
Admiralty B2 · 12 sources · refreshed 2026-04-17 06:00 UTC
Every claim travels with its confidence — Admiralty grade, source count, timestamp. Tabular numerals. Lowercase 'refreshed'.
Don't — Omit provenance
Data refreshed recently.
Vague timestamps, no source count, no Admiralty grade. A CRQ claim without provenance is inadmissible.
Do — Navy circle, single letter
M W C G S
M · W · C · G · S. Five categories. Filled navy. No other icons, no pictograms, no emoji.
Don't — Decorative iconography
⚠🌊🛡️🔒📦
Emoji and illustrative icons undermine the report tone and render inconsistently across PDF / PPTX pipelines.
Do — White cards, rule dividers
APAC · SCENARIO 01 OF 3
Taiwan Strait — sustained chokepoint pressure
Structure comes from rules and white cards. Shadows are minimal; no colored backgrounds behind body copy.
Don't — Tinted severity backgrounds
Taiwan Strait — sustained chokepoint pressure
Severity tints and accent-border cards read as alert-UI, not board reporting. The pill is enough.
